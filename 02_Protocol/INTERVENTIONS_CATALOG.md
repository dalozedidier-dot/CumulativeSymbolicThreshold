# Intervention and control catalog

Symbolic interventions:
1) Remove access to repertoire
2) Decodification
3) Reduce transmission density
4) Reduce fidelity
5) Increase transmission costs

Controls:
- Negative controls that do not change the symbolic channel
- Positive controls expected to increase transmission

Record each intervention with date, window [t-Δ, t], intensity, expected mechanism, protocol, and outcome.
